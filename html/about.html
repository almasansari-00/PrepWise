About PrepWise

PrepWise is an Interview Preparation Platform designed to help students and aspiring developers prepare for technical interviews. The platform provides interview questions, quizzes, mock interviews, learning progress tracking, and a personalized dashboard.

Features

Interview Questions

Practice topic-wise interview questions.

Quiz

Test your knowledge with timed quizzes.

Mock Interview

Improve confidence with interview simulations.

Progress Tracking

Monitor your interview preparation journey.

Developer

PrepWise is developed as a full-stack interview preparation project to help students improve their technical interview skills.
